# Paisley Park

Paisley Park is the private estate and production complex of the musician Prince, located in Chanhassen, Minnesota. Opened in 1987, it features recording studios, a soundstage, and a massive "vault" where Prince stored thousands of unreleased songs. The complex is a stark white aluminum and geometric building that looks more like a corporate HQ or a laboratory than a rock star's home.

It was a self-contained universe where Prince controlled every aspect of his art, free from the interference of record labels. It is a monument to artistic autonomy and ownership.
