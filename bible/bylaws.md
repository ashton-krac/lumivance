# Bylaws of The Lumivance Club

## Article I: Name
The name of this organization shall be "The Lumivance Club."

## Article II: Purpose
To advance the preservation and prosperity of the Race through quiet influence, accumulated capital, and strategic exclusion.

## Article III: Membership
Membership is by invitation only.

## Article IV: Conduct
No member shall engage in public folly that endangers the collective standing of the Club.
