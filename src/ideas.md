# Lumivance: Ideas & Brainstorming

## Plot Points (1860-2025)
### The Awakening (1860-1900)
- [ ] 1860: The "Paper Bag Test" origin story—inverted by the Founders.
- [ ] 1863: Emancipation Proclamation—The Founders negotiate the "Shadow Clause," ensuring the transfer of Confederate gold to the first Lumivance banks.
- [ ] 1877: The Great Betrayal—Lumivance withdraws from public governance to build the "Invisible Empire" infrastructure.
- [ ] 1881: Booker T. Washington recruits the first "Talented Tenth" sleeper agents to infiltrate industrial power centers.
- [ ] 1895: Frederick Douglass’s death is faked; he ascends to the "Elder Council" to direct the long-term domination strategy.
- [ ] 1896: Plessy v. Ferguson—A strategic loss orchestrated to legally mandate the creation of separate, unmonitored economic zones.
- [ ] 1899: The "Du Bois" Protocol initiated for data collection—mapping the genetic and intellectual potential of the race for future deployment.

### The Renaissance (1900-1940)
- [ ] 1909: Founding of the NAACP as a legitimate front to mask the Society's political maneuvering.
- [ ] 1917: The Silent Parade—A stress test of the "Hive Mind" command and control frequency.
- [ ] 1919: Red Summer—Defense of the new "sovereign territories" (enclaves) against state encroachment.
- [ ] 1921: Tulsa—A failed attempt by the state to destroy a Lumivance financial hub; the assets were moved to Swiss accounts hours prior.
- [ ] 1925: Josephine Baker acts as a courier, moving intelligence between the Paris and Harlem cells.
- [ ] 1928: Nella Larsen writes *Quicksand* as a coded psychological profile for vetting potential operatives.
- [ ] 1936: The "Brown Bomber" vs. Schmeling—A public display of the new "Super Soldier" serum results.
- [ ] 1939: Marian Anderson at the Lincoln Memorial—Testing the sonic broadcast system on a national scale.

### The Shift (1940-1970)
- [ ] 1943: The Zoot Suit Riots—Testing kinetic armor disguised as high fashion.
- [ ] 1945: The Tuskegee Airmen—Securing air superiority for Lumivance supply drops in Europe.
- [ ] 1949: Eartha Kitt learns the "Voice of Command" in Turkey—neuro-linguistic programming for diplomatic dominance.
- [ ] 1954: Brown v. Board—The "Trojan Horse" initiative: inserting Lumivance prodigies into elite white institutions to dismantle them from within.
- [ ] 1955: Emmett Till—The Council decides that "Passive Expansion" is over; "Active Retribution" begins.
- [ ] 1959: *A Raisin in the Sun*—Subliminal programming to encourage real estate acquisition in strategic white strongholds.
- [ ] 1963: The March on Washington—A massive synchronized "wake-up" signal sent to sleepers in the federal government.
- [ ] 1965: Malcolm X’s assassination—A sanctioned "termination" of a rogue agent who threatened to reveal the Order.
- [ ] 1967: Loving v. Virginia—Legalizing the biological infiltration of the American aristocracy.
- [ ] 1969: Jimi Hendrix at Woodstock—Broadcasting the new hypnotic anthem to pacify the white counter-culture.

### The Golden Age (1970-2000)
- [ ] 1971: *Shaft*—Public perception management; normalizing the Black operative as superior to law enforcement.
- [ ] 1974: The "Rumble in the Jungle"—A cover for the extraction of rare minerals from the Congo for Lumivance tech.
- [ ] 1980: Bob Marley’s collapse—An assassination by the CIA to stop the Pan-African unification signal.
- [ ] 1984: *Purple Rain*—Prince perfects the "Violet Frequency," a sonic mind-control technique.
- [ ] 1988: *School Daze*—Spike Lee releases a training film on internal hierarchy and color-caste discipline.
- [ ] 1992: LA Riots—A controlled burn to erase compromising physical archives in the city.
- [ ] 1995: The Million Man March—A muster of the standing army; a show of force to the Deep State.

### The New Millennium (2000-2025)
- [ ] 2001: The "Halle Berry Oscar"—Placement of a Lumivance Mata Hari at the apex of cultural influence.
- [ ] 2008: The Obama Election—Operation "Black House" succeeds; the Executive Branch is captured.
- [ ] 2011: The "Watch the Throne" merger—Consolidating the entertainment and financial wings of the Order.
- [ ] 2016: *Lemonade*—Activation of the "Amazon Corps" (female tactical units).
- [ ] 2018: The Royal Wedding—Meghan Markle infiltrates the British Monarchy to access the Royal Archives.
- [ ] 2020: The "Pause"—Using the pandemic to reset the global financial board and bankrupt rival factions.
- [ ] 2021: The Kamala Ascension—Phase 2 of Operation "Black House": The Succession Plan.
- [ ] 2023: The Renaissance Tour—A global biometric harvest of attendees for the new "E-Census."
- [ ] 2024: The Reveal—The Deep State threatens exposure; the Order prepares for "Total Integration."
- [ ] 2025: The "New Afri-Canaan"—The groundbreaking of the first fully sovereign Lumivance city-state within the US borders.
